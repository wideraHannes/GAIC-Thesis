        <H1>Curve Skeleton Motion Synthesis</H1>
        
          <H2>Representation</H2>
          In our method, the parameter t on the curve plays an important role in the deformation.
We use B-splines to represent the curve skeleton.
        
        
          <H2>Local Frame Def|nition</H2>
          Similar to the joint-based skeleton, each point on the curve in a curve skeleton has a local frame (similar to a Frenet frame) defining the space transformation sampled at that point.
