In this dataset, an argument is a sentence or segment in online dialogue that clearly expresses a particular argument facet, meaning a substantive position or reasoning relevant to a debated topic that can be interpreted as making a case. Argumentative sentences are those that express an argument which is understandable and interpretable on its own or with minimal contextual inference, and that articulates a stance, justification, or challenge within the dialogue. The dataset focuses on extracting such focused argument segments rather than entire posts, emphasizing clarity and the ability to reconstruct the argument without extensive additional context. Sentences that do not express an argument, or whose argument cannot be reconstructed without significantly more context, are treated as non-arguments. Utterances that are not making an argument at all, such as insults or purely dialogic remarks, are explicitly considered non-argumentative.
