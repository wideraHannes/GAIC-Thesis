        <H1>1 Introduction</H1>
      
      In this paper we describe a technique for animating the behavior of materials, such as the one shown in Figure 1 , that exhibit a combination of both fluid and solid characteristics.
Often referred to as viscoelastic fluids, these materials initially respond to strain elastically like a solid, but when subjected to increasingly large stresses they flow like a fluid.
