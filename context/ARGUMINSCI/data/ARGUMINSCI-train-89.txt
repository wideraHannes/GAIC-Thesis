During swing initiation, constant excitation values between 0 and 1 (set during optimization) are added and subtracted to the VAS, RF, GLU, and HFL: The combination of HFL and GLU excitations creates a large hip flexion torque, while the VAS and RF excitations effectively allow the optimizer to adjust the initial knee swing angle and velocity.
