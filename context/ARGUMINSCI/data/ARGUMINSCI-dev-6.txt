        <H1>1 Introduction</H1>
      
      Animation of skin and muscular deformations of human characters and other living creatures has long been one of the most important applications of deformable modeling in computer graphics, notably in feature animation and more recently in increasingly realistic computer games and interactive medical and training applications.
