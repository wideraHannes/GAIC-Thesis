Stimulated whole (SWS) and parotid (SPS) saliva flow were measured and Medical Outcomes Short Form 36 (SF-36), European Organization for Research and Treatment of Cancer (EORTC) core quetionnaire, and EORTC head-and-neck module (QLQ-H&amp;N35) were completed at baseline and 2, 6, and 12 months after radiotherapy.
Forty-six patients (88%) were in disease remission 12 months after radiotherapy.
