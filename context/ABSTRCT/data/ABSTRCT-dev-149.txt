The hazard ratio of death for patients receiving the combination treatment was 1.17 (95% confidence interval [CI] = 0.95 to 1.44) that of patients receiving vinorelbine and 1.06 (95% CI = 0.86 to 1.29) that of patients receiving gemcitabine.
Although quality of life was similar across the three treatment arms, the combination treatment was more toxic than the two drugs given singly.
