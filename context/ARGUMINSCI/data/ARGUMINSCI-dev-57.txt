Mass-PCA combines and compacts the U i , retaining only k most significant modes.
Finally, we explicitly add morph target deformations x 0 i to the eigenbasis and and guarantee massorthogonality of the final basis U.

        By using pose-space efficient polynomial interpolation to achieve pose-dependent behavior, we are able to demonstrate rich nonlinear deformation effects at relatively small extra cost compared to simple simulation of linear or semi-non-linear materials.
