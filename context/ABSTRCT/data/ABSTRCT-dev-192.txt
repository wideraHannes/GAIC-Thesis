Patients were randomized in a 2-by-2 factorial design to medroxyprogesterone acetate 200 mg daily, interferon-alfa 9 million IU 3 times a week, subcutaneous interleukin-2 9 million IU daily, or a combination of both cytokines.
Tumor response was evaluated at Week 12 and Month 6; progression-free patients received further identical treatment for a maximum of 3 additional months.
