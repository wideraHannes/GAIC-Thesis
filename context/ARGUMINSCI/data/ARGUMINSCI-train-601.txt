Skeletal subspace deformation (SSD)</H2>
        SSD [MTLT88] is based on the weighted blending of an affine transformation of each joint by equation 2.
        
          2
          n joint v a = S(v 0 ) = ( ∑ w j T j )v 0 j=1
        
        where n joint is the number of joints, v a is a vertex in an arbitrary pose p a , v 0 is a vertex in the rest pose, and w j is a joint weight that defines the contribution of joint j’s transformations to the current vertex.
