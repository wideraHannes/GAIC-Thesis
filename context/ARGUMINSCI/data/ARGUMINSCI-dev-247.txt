        <H1>3 Linear Blend Skinning</H1>
        The input to LBS consists of a polygonal mesh representing the digital skin, a skeleton, and vertex weights for every vertex of the skin.
The polygonal mesh and the skeleton are designed in a reference position, e.g. virtual characters are often posed in the da Vinci posture 
[Steed 2002].
