In regions without a substantial heating demand an increase in total building energy consumption was found, and when a substantial heating load was present a slight increase or decrease in energy consumption was observed.
Changes in total building energy consumption of between −0.6% and +8.3%, and an increase in the total design cooling equipment capacity of 9.1%–25.0% was predicted over the period 1990 to 2080 due to climate change for the different cities.
