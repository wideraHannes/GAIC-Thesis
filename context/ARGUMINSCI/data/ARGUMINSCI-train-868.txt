          <H2>4.1 Surface Tension</H2>
          To incorporate surface tension, we follow Enright et al. [2003] in setting the free surface pressure p fs = p air + γκ fs , where p air is the constant air pressure, γ is the surface tension coefficient and κ fs is the mean curvature of the surface.
