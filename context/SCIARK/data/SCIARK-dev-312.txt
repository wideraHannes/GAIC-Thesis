Title: Mental health and well-being concerns of fly-in fly-out workers and their partners in Australia: a qualitative study
Abstract
Objectives: Fly-in fly-out (FIFO) work involves commuting long distances to the worksite and living in provided accommodation for 1-4 weeks while on shift.
While the potentially detrimental impact of FIFO work on the health and well-being of workers has been documented, little attention has been paid to how workers, or their partners, cope with this impact.
