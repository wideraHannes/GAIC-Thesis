The scores of TCM efficacy (24.52 +/- 8.98 vs 29.76 +/- 7.09, P < 0.05) and the 6-min walking distance (233.4 +/- 29.2 m vs 205.8 +/- 31.9 m, P < 0.05) were better in the treated group.
After treatment in the treated group, the body weight was increased in 11 patients (20.0%), stabilized in 28 (50.9%) and decreased in 16 (29.1%), while in the control group it was increased in 5 (9.4%), stabilized in 26 (49.1%) and decreased in 22 (41.5%), difference between the two groups was significant, too (P < 0.05).
