The prevalence of acute IOP rise (>21 mmHg) was significantly higher in placebo group compared with clonidine group (25.8% vs 9.6%; P=0.091).
Administering preoperative oral clonidine in a dosage of 5g/kg, 2h before phacoemulsification, significantly decreases the prevalence and intensity of acute postoperative IOP rise in those undergoing general anesthesia.
