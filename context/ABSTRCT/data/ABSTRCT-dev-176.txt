All regimens produced clinically relevant and statistically significant (P<.05) intraocular pressure reductions from baseline.
Mean changes in intraocular pressure trough measurements ranged from -2.7 to -3.9 mm Hg with brinzolamide twice-daily dosing and -2.8 to -3.8 mm Hg three times daily dosing compared with -4.7 to -5.6 mm Hg with timolol.
