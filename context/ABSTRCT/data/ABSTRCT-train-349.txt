The mean IOP reduction was significantly greater in the BTFC group than in the LTFC group (-21.4% vs -13.7%, p<0.001).
A higher percentage of patients in the BTFC group showed a mean IOP reduction from baseline >or=15% (72.3% vs 40.0%) and >or=20% (61.7% vs 17.1%) compared to patients in the LTFC group.
