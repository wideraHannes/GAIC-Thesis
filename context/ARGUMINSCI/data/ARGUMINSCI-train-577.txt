As shown in Figure 5 the lower body consists of pelvis, upper leg, lower leg, foot, and toes, and they are connected at the hip, knee, ankle, and ball joints.
The total degree of freedom of the lower body is 8 3 + 6 = 30 . (All the joints were modeled by 3-DOF joints, and the base has extra 6 DOFs.) The destination character was about 60% scaled down from the source character with non-uniform proportions.
