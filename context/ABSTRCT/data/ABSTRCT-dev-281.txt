The 1-, 2-, and 3-year survival rates were 88.9%, 44.4%, and 16.7% for the vaccine group, respectively, and 75.0%, 18.8%, and 0%, respectively, for the control group, (P = 0.299, 0.0035, 0.0014, respectively).
The median OS for the vaccine group was 31.9 months and for the control group was 15.0 months (P < 0.002).
