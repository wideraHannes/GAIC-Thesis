Popović and Witkin [1999]
 have shown that significant changes to motion capture data can be made by optimizing with a physically based objective function when the character is reduced to the degrees of freedom most important for the task.
When physics does not dominate the motion, kinematic techniques can give the animator interactive control for motion editing (e.g., 
[Gleicher 1997] [Lee and Shin 1999] [Arikan and Forsyth 2002]).
